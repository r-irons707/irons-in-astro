About Me

Hello! I am a fourth year in the Astronomical Sciences Specialist at UofT. You can find me roaming around campus or in a hidden study space. I frequent any conference or talk held at UofT. Most recently has been the Scintillometry Workshop, but I have enjoyed the CITA 2022 Planet Day and RandoAstro this summer past. I enjoy filling my time learning new things (hence this new website) and expanding my skill set (my next project will most likely be CSS and JS to spice up this HTML file, unless I feel like keeping this astro prof. drip/style, iykyk). In the future I hope to get into some research and apply everything I have compiled throughout my undergrad. Currently I spend my time reading papers and doing personal research along with my school work.

Education: UofT Undergraduate student, Astronomical Sciences Specialist, Physics Major, Mathematics Minor

Research Interests: I am interested in anything astronomy! I particularly enjoy learning about stars, pulsars, and working with simulations. I would love to delve right in and start applying more of my knowledge outside of lab courses and personal projects.

Community and Involvement

Teaching: Currently I teach high school physics, mathematics, and chemistry with Tutorbright.

Outreach:

  • AstroTours

    • AstroTours is a public outreach event run by the David. A. Dunlap group. I work as a demonstration volunteer with telescopes and informative tables to educate the public. It is ran the first Thursday of every month in MP tower.

  • Perseid Meteor Shower Excursion

    • I was one of two organizers for this event to Earl Rowe Provincial Park where we had UofT students and members of the public view the Perseid Meteor Shower on August 12, 2022. We had around 70 people in attendance, and was part of a joint event ran by UTMPC and UTAAS.

  • UofT Amateur Astronomer's Society (UTAAS) President (2022-2023)

    • As part of this position I run the general program of the club. This includes event logistics and management.

  • UofT at Mississauga Physics Club (UTMPC) Event Director (2022-2023)

    • Pitch and run events hosted by UTMPC.

  • UofT at Mississauga Physics Club (UTMPC) Event Director Associate (2021-2022)

    • Assist the Event Director in creation and planning of events hosted by UTMPC.
